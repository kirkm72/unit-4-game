Crystal Collector

This game will generate and show a magic number. There will be 4 crystals shown. Each crystal will be worth a random number between 1 & 12. Selecting any crystal will add the random number assigned to that crystal to your number. Select any combination of crytals that will result in adding your number up to the magic number without going over. Go over and you lose.
